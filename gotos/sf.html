my go-to's in san francisco

  1. Get a $4 cappuccino at The Coffee Movement
  2. Run the Golden Mile at Golden Gate Park
  3. Enjoy a fancy cocktail and mushrooms at True Laurel
  4. Order a super burrito (El Dorado style) from La Taqueria
  5. Order pan perdue at Verjus
  6. Get a walk-in table at San Ho Won at 4:45pm
  7. Get al pastor tacos at Leo's Tacos Truck by Ocean Beach
  8. Hangout in the park at Presidio Tunnel Tops
  9. Get a croissant at Arsicault Bakery
  10. Visit the Ferry Building and have some bites
