my go-to's in new orleans

New Orleans is a city full of life and Americana. It's the birthplace of some of the greatest classic cocktails, the genre of jazz, and Creole & Cajun cuisine. Like Vegas, it's also a great time as a 48hr trip with your friends.

  1. Get a Ramos Gin Fizz at The Sazerac Bar
  2. See a show at Preservation Hall
  3. Enjoy a fancy cocktail and apps at Jewel of the South
  4. Get a crawfish boil at Cajun Seafood
  5. Get fresh, hot beignets at Cafe Du Monde
  6. Walk down Royal Street during the day and admire the architecture
  7. Go see jazz at Mahogany Jazz Hall
  8. Go see the National WWII Museum
